Metaphors

Notes
A metaphor is a figure of speech that compares two unrelated things by stating that one thing is another, without using "like" or "as."

Metaphors are used to create vivid imagery and convey abstract ideas in a more relatable or emotional way.

Extended metaphors continue the comparison throughout a piece of writing, developing the metaphor over multiple sentences or paragraphs.

Metaphors are common in everyday language, literature, and poetry, enhancing expression and helping readers or listeners understand complex concepts
